About Us

JS-Romumetalli Oy is a modern scrap metal collection and recycling company located in Köyliö. We are dedicated to responsible recycling, offering services to both individuals and businesses. Our goal is to ensure efficient, ecological and customer-oriented service every step of the way.

With years of industry experience and reliable logistics, we ensure transparent pricing and professional service across Satakunta and beyond.

Our Services

Metal Collection

We collect various types of scrap metal from construction sites, households, and industrial facilities.

Scrap Yard

Our Köyliö-based scrap yard accepts all types of ferrous and non-ferrous metals.

Container Rental

We provide rental containers for metal waste, with delivery and pickup at your location.

Vehicle Recycling

We responsibly dispose of and recycle end-of-life vehicles with proper documentation.
