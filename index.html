About JS-Romumetalli

JS-Romumetalli Oy is a Finnish company specializing in the recycling and dismantling of old vehicles and scrap metal. With years of experience and a strong commitment to sustainability, we provide efficient, eco-friendly, and professional services across Finland.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable solutions in metal recycling while delivering high-quality service to our clients and partners.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✔️ Fast vehicle removal
  • ✔️ Environmentally responsible processing
  • ✔️ Competitive pricing
  • ✔️ Trusted by hundreds of customers

Our Services
